We Offer

  • 01 Data Analytics

    Our web application can generate data visualizations of the traffic incident reports for Task Force for Transport and Traffic Management (TF-TTM) to assist them in better visualizing the data and providing solutions.

  • 02 Better Assistance

    Using this web application, TF-TTM may quickly and efficiently develop solutions. Additionally, it will assist Quezon City TF-TTM in preventing traffic incidents.

  • 03 Public User's Convenience

    Whenever they encounter a traffic incident, motorists, commuters, pedestrians, students, professionals, and others can submit a report. This will make it easier for TF-TTM to identify the incident's location and specifics.
